Section 2.1 — Core palette

Three color families: navy (canvas), green (accent), blue (secondary). All values are pulled directly from the live :root in css/styles.css.

Navy family — primary canvas

--navy#0B1D38
Primary canvas
Hero background, final CTA section, primary button gradient start, footer.
--navy-l#15294D
Navy lighter
Hover state for navy backgrounds. Mid-tone in primary button gradient.
--navy-xl#1E3562
Navy extra-light
Primary button gradient end. The "lifted" navy you see on .btn-primary.
linear-gradient(135deg, --navy → --navy-xl)
Navy gradient
.btn-primary background. The "Get a free quote" nav button.

Sage green family — primary accent

--green#2D7A32
Primary accent (sage)
CTAs (.btn-sage), section labels (.sec-lbl), links on light, active states, italic underlines.
--green-l#3D9A43
Sage lighter
Hover state for green. Section labels on dark backgrounds (.sec-lbl-l).
--gbgrgba(45,122,50,.07)
Sage 7% tint
.sec-lbl pill background. Subtle sage tint behind eyebrows.
--gbdrgba(45,122,50,.18)
Sage 18% tint
.sec-lbl pill border. The thin sage outline on eyebrow chips.

Electric blue family — secondary accent (never primary)

--blue#3068AD
Secondary accent
Focus rings (--focus-ring), text links (when not sage), hovers. Never a primary CTA.
--blue-l#4A82C7
Blue lighter
Blue hover state. Mid-stop in hero's 3px footer gradient line.

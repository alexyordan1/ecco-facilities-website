About Ecco

Clean, held to a higher standard.

Ecco is a New York City commercial cleaning company built to close a simple gap — cleaning that's premium and dependable, yet genuinely safe for the people who use the space and the planet we share. Eco-certified products on every job, dedicated teams, and pricing you can actually read.

Why we exist

Premium and responsible — not one or the other.

In New York, commercial cleaning too often forces a choice: cheap and harsh, or premium and impersonal. We built Ecco to refuse that trade-off.

Every product we use is eco-certified — Green Seal or EPA Safer Choice — so a spotless space never comes at the cost of the air your team breathes. And because we supply everything and price every job transparently, you get a partner that's genuinely easy to work with, not one more vendor to manage.

We keep staffing consistent, hold our work to a documented standard, and stand behind it with a re-clean guarantee. The goal is simple: a space that looks, feels, and is cleaner — for everyone who walks through it.

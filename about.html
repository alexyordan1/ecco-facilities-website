Born from Experience, Built on Principle

Before Ecco existed, our founders Alex and Bianca spent years working inside the facility services industry in New York. They managed crews, handled client accounts, and saw firsthand how the business worked from the inside — the good and the bad.

What they saw too often was an industry that treated cleaning as a commodity. Companies that cut corners to protect margins. Crews that rotated constantly so no one ever learned a client's space. Products chosen for cost, not safety. And a culture where the client only heard from their provider when something went wrong.

Ecco was founded on the belief that facility services should be done differently. That the people cleaning your space should know it as well as you do. That the products used inside your facility should be safe enough for your children, your pets, and your most vulnerable employees. And that your service provider should feel like a partner — not a vendor you have to chase.

Over 12 years later, that belief hasn't changed. It's become our standard.

These Aren't Slogans. They're How We Operate.

People Over Chemicals

Every product we use is Green Seal certified, non-toxic, and biodegradable. This wasn't a marketing decision — it was a moral one. We will never use a product that puts anyone's health at risk, period.

Consistency Over Promises

Anyone can promise a great first clean. What separates Ecco is the 200th clean being identical to the first. We achieve this through dedicated teams, detailed checklists, and regular quality inspections.

Relationships Over Transactions

We don't believe in anonymous service. When you work with Ecco, you know your team by name, and they know your space inside out. We pick up the phone. We solve problems the same day.

Transparency Over Fine Print

Our contracts are straightforward. Our pricing is clear. If something isn't right, we fix it at no charge — no excuses, no runaround.

Great Service Doesn't Happen by Accident

1

Facility Assessment

Before we quote anything, we visit your space. We assess the layout, traffic patterns, high-touch areas, and specific requirements.

2

Custom Service Plan

Based on the assessment, we build a detailed service plan: what gets cleaned, how often, with which products, and on what schedule.

3

Dedicated Team Assignment

We assign a consistent team to your facility. No random crew rotations. Your team is your team.

4

Service Checklists & Documentation

Every visit follows a detailed checklist specific to your facility. Nothing gets missed because nothing is left to memory.

5

Quality Inspections

We conduct regular quality inspections — both scheduled and unannounced — to verify that our standards are being met.

6

Client Feedback Loop

You always have a direct line to your account manager. If something isn't right, we re-clean at no charge.

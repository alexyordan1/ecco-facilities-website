### 3 Spring Cleaning Tips for NYC Offices

1. **Deep clean high-touch surfaces** — Door handles, elevator buttons, and shared kitchen appliances harbor more germs than you'd think. A weekly deep-clean schedule keeps illness rates down.

2. **Swap to eco-certified products** — Green Seal and EPA Safer Choice products deliver the same cleaning power without the harsh chemicals. Your team breathes easier, literally.

3. **Don't forget the windows** — Natural light boosts productivity by up to 15%. Clean windows make a bigger difference than most office managers realize.

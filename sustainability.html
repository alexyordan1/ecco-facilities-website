Spotless — without the chemical cost.

A clean space shouldn't come at the expense of the air your team breathes. Every product we use is eco-certified, and the way we work is built to waste less — so your facility is genuinely cleaner, for the people in it and the city around it.

Eco-certified, every job

Not a marketing word — a certification.

Every product we use carries an independent eco-certification: Green Seal or EPA Safer Choice. We supply it all, so you never have to think about what's being used in your building.

These aren't labels we print ourselves. They're third-party standards that vet a product's ingredients, performance, and impact — which means a spotless result without the harsh residue or chemical odor that ordinary cleaners leave behind.

What that means in practice

  • Biodegradable and low-VOC — fewer fumes, less residue
  • Safe for staff, visitors, children, and pets
  • Gentler on people with allergies and sensitivities
  • Supplied by us — turnkey, on every visit

How we keep it green

Greener in every detail.

It's not just the products. It's how we clean — chosen to waste less and protect the air inside your space.

Reusable microfiber

We clean with microfiber cloths and mops that are laundered and reused — capturing more dust and grime with far less water and fewer chemicals than disposables.

Concentrated, low-waste products

Concentrated formulas with controlled dilution mean less packaging, less plastic, and less waste shipped and stored — without watering down the result.

HEPA-filtration vacuums

Our vacuums use HEPA filtration to trap fine dust and allergens instead of recirculating them — so it's the air in your space getting cleaner, not just the floors.

Recycling & waste sorting

We help your building separate and divert waste correctly — so more gets recycled and less ends up in a landfill, visit after visit.

No harsh residue or odor

Low-VOC, eco-certified products leave a space that smells clean because it is — not masked under a heavy chemical scent that lingers for hours.

Safe for sensitive spaces

The same approach makes a real difference where it matters most — medical and dental offices, schools and daycares, and anywhere people are more sensitive.

A lighter footprint

A spotless space — and a lighter footprint to go with it.

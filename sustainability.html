The Hidden Problem

The products cleaning your facility right now might be making people sick.

Most commercial cleaning products contain VOCs, ammonia, chlorine, and synthetic fragrances that linger in the air and settle on surfaces. For people with asthma, allergies, eczema, or respiratory conditions — and for children, elderly individuals, and pets — the impact is serious.

Ecco was founded to eliminate this problem entirely.

Our Approach

100% Eco-Certified. Every Product. Every Visit. Since Day One.

When Alex and Bianca founded Ecco, they made a decision that has never changed: every product must be independently certified as safe. The two green leaves in our logo represent this commitment. They have since day one, over 12 years ago.

Accessibility Statement

Last updated: April 1, 2026

Our Commitment

Ecco Facilities LLC is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We continually improve the user experience for everyone and apply the relevant accessibility standards to make our website as accessible as possible.

Conformance Status

We aim to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA. These guidelines explain how to make web content more accessible for people with disabilities and more user-friendly for everyone.

Measures We Take

Ecco Facilities LLC takes the following measures to ensure the accessibility of our website:

  • Semantic HTML — We use proper heading hierarchy, landmarks, and ARIA attributes throughout the site.
  • Keyboard navigation — All interactive elements are accessible via keyboard. A visible focus indicator is provided on all focusable elements.
  • Skip navigation — A "Skip to main content" link is provided at the top of every page for keyboard and screen reader users.
  • Color contrast — We maintain sufficient color contrast ratios between text and background colors throughout the site.
  • Alternative text — All meaningful images include descriptive alternative text.
  • Reduced motion — Animations and parallax effects are automatically disabled for users who have indicated a preference for reduced motion in their operating system settings.
  • Responsive design — Our site adapts to different screen sizes and is functional on mobile devices, tablets, and desktops.
  • Form accessibility — All form fields include proper labels, error messages, and ARIA attributes for screen reader compatibility.
  • No auto-play media — We do not use auto-playing audio or video content.

Technologies Used

The accessibility of this website relies on the following technologies:

  • HTML5
  • CSS3
  • JavaScript
  • WAI-ARIA

Known Limitations

Despite our best efforts, some content on our website may not yet be fully accessible. We are aware of the following limitations and are working to address them:

  • Third-party content — Some third-party tools and widgets (such as embedded maps, chat features, or payment processors) may not be fully accessible. We work with vendors to improve compatibility where possible.
  • PDF documents — Some downloadable documents may not be optimized for screen readers. Contact us for accessible alternatives.

Feedback

We welcome your feedback on the accessibility of the Ecco Facilities LLC website. If you encounter accessibility barriers or have suggestions for improvement, please contact us:

  • Email: info@eccofacilities.com
  • Subject line: Website Accessibility Feedback

We aim to respond to accessibility feedback within 2 business days and to resolve issues within 10 business days.

Enforcement Procedure

If you are not satisfied with our response to your accessibility concern, you may file a complaint with the relevant enforcement authority in your jurisdiction. In the United States, the Department of Justice Civil Rights Division handles ADA-related digital accessibility matters.

Assessment and Review

This accessibility statement was last reviewed on April 1, 2026. We conduct periodic accessibility reviews and update this statement accordingly. Our assessment approach includes:

  • Automated accessibility testing using Lighthouse and axe-core
  • Manual keyboard navigation testing
  • Screen reader testing with VoiceOver
  • Review of color contrast ratios

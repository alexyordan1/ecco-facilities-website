Why eco-friendly matters

A clean space should never come at the expense of your team.

Conventional commercial cleaners use chemistry that's effective on surfaces but harsh on the people who live in the space afterward. We use products that meet the same effectiveness bar without the trade-off.

Safe for sensitive employees

No residue, no harsh fragrance bombs, no fumes that linger in HVAC. Asthma, allergies, chemical sensitivity — your team won't notice the cleaning, only the clean.

Safe during pregnancy

EPA Safer Choice products avoid known endocrine disruptors and reproductive toxins. Pregnant employees don't have to leave the floor when the crew arrives.

Safe for pets & kids

Frequently asked

Eco-cleaning questions, answered.

What does Green Seal certified actually mean?

Green Seal is an independent, third-party certification verifying products meet strict health and environmental standards. Certified products must demonstrate reduced toxicity, biodegradability, low VOCs, and minimal environmental impact. Our entire product set is Green Seal or EPA Safer Choice certified — no exceptions.

Is eco-friendly cleaning actually as effective?

Yes — and in many cases more effective. Certified products kill the same pathogens as harsh conventional cleaners without the residue, fumes, or skin irritation. Our crews are trained on the right product for each surface — you get a deeper clean, not a weaker one.

Will it cost more than conventional cleaning?

No. Our pricing is competitive with standard commercial cleaning services. Certified products cost slightly more per gallon but use less per application — net cost difference is negligible. You pay for cleaning quality, not chemistry shortcuts.

Does this help with LEED certification?

Yes. Our service contributes to LEED EB credits for Green Cleaning (EQ Credit 3.1–3.6) including Green Seal certified products, microfiber technology, and sustainable cleaning equipment. Documentation provided for your LEED submission on request.

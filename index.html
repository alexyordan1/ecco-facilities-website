Before you ask

Questions facility managers ask us first.

Are you insured? Can you provide a certificate of insurance?

Yes. Ecco is fully insured: general liability, workers' compensation, commercial auto, and a $5M umbrella policy. We provide a certificate of insurance (COI), and can name your building or management company as additional insured, on request.

Which products do you use? Are they safe for people and pets?

We clean with Green Seal and EPA Safer Choice certified products: biodegradable, low-VOC, and safe for use around staff, patients, children, and pets. The same spotless result, without the harsh chemical residue.

Can you work after hours or around our schedule?

Yes. Commercial Cleaning can happen after you close, so your space is ready before anyone arrives, and a day porter covers daytime upkeep. We build the schedule around how your facility actually runs.

How do you keep quality consistent?

You get a dedicated, trained team (the same people who learn your space), backed by quality inspections, a direct line to ownership, and our re-clean guarantee: if something isn't right, tell us within 24 hours and we re-clean it at no charge.

What does it cost, and how fast can we start?

Every proposal is custom and flat-rate, with no generic packages and no hidden fees. Tell us about your space and you'll have a transparent proposal within 24 hours on business days, with no obligation. Once approved, your team can be on-site as soon as your scope allows, often within days.

What if we're switching from another cleaning company?

We handle the transition for you: we review your current scope, build a plan tailored to your space, and onboard your dedicated team so nothing is dropped in the handoff. And there's no multi-year lock-in, just a straightforward agreement you can step away from.

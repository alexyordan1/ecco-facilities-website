Why Ecco

Work you can be proud of.

We're not the cheapest cleaner in the city, and we're not trying to be. We do it right — and we look after the people who make that happen.

Work that means something

We don't cut corners. You'll be part of a team doing premium, eco-conscious cleaning the right way — and taking real pride in how a space looks when you're done.

Train and grow

We invest in our people. You'll be trained properly on your space and our standards — and there's real room to take on more and grow as you do.

Safer for you, too

Every product we use is eco-certified — no harsh chemicals or heavy fumes. That means a healthier day for the people doing the cleaning, not just for our clients.

The work · day to day

Real spaces. A real standard.

This is hands-on work in real New York City spaces — offices, lobbies, and the common areas people use every day. You're set up to do it right, not rushed.

You won't be handed a cart and left to figure it out. We train you on your space and the way we do things, then trust you to hold the line on quality.

What you can expect

  • Proper training on your space and our standards before you're on your own
  • Eco-certified products only — no harsh chemicals or heavy fumes to breathe
  • Premium spaces across New York City, kept to a high standard
  • Real pride in the result — the kind you can see when a space looks right

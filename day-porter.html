Before you ask

Day porter questions.

How is a day porter different from Commercial Cleaning?

A day porter works on-site during your business hours, keeping things clean throughout the day while the space is in use. Commercial Cleaning is a complete reset of the whole facility, on a schedule you set. Many clients run both: daytime presence plus a complete reset.

How many hours and how often?

Entirely up to you. Coverage is custom: anything from a few hours on your busiest days to a full-time presence, with as many porters as your space needs. No fixed packages; we build it around your operation.

Is it the same porter each time?

We keep staffing consistent, so your porter becomes a familiar, dedicated presence who learns your building and your standards, not a rotating crew. They're background-checked and trained before they ever step on-site.

What products do you use?

Eco-certified cleaning products, Green Seal and EPA Safer Choice, that we supply. Biodegradable, low-VOC, and safe for use around staff, visitors, children, and pets, with no harsh chemical odors. Where disinfection is required, we use EPA-registered disinfectants.

What kinds of spaces do you cover?

Because a day porter's scope adapts, the service fits almost any occupied, high-traffic space: offices, coworking, medical, retail, gyms, residential lobbies, restaurant front-of-house, multi-tenant common areas, and more.

Are you insured?

Yes. Ecco is fully insured: general liability, workers' compensation, commercial auto, and a $5M umbrella policy. We provide a certificate of insurance (COI) and can name your building or management company as additional insured, on request.
